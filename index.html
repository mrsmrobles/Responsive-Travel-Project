Why San Diego?

San Diego, the birthplace of California

Widely recognized as “America’s Finest City,” San Diego is famous for gorgeous weather, miles of white-sand beaches, and some of the nation’s most popular tourist spots. This city is considered the birthplace of California, becoming part of the U.S. during 1848 with the Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo, and is now the eighth-largest city in the country. Renowned for its pleasant year-round climate, San Diego draws tourists from around the world due to its exciting variety of popular gardens, parks, museums, and cultural attractions. With gorgeous scenery and endless entertainment options, San Diego is a nationally beloved treasure in Southern California. Whether you want to check out an art exhibit, take a safari tour, learn how to surf, ride roller coasters, or simply relax and enjoy the striking beauty of the coast, there is something fun for everyone in San Diego.
